Concert Poster

For this project in my Graphic Design for the Web course, I created an 18"x24" Swiss-style concert poster inspired by a Lana Del Rey show I wish I had attended. The design features a bold red, cream, and blue color palette and uses a clean sans-serif typeface to reflect Swiss design principles such as strong grid alignment, minimalism, and typographic hierarchy.
