Survivor Season 45

As survivor is one of my favorite shows its hard for me to not like a season. With that being said season 45 was not the survivor I grew up watching. While it ended strong with good players, the beggining was ROUGH to watch. In my opinion many of the people that first left the show were players that volentarily left because it was just too hard. Survivor is a once in a lifetime chance to get casted on, they should'nt be casting people that leave just becuase they miss their bed! I want the old, nitty-gritty, non new era type players back.
