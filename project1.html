Personal Logo

This project was for my Graphic Design for the Web course. I was assigned to focus on creating a personal logo as the foundation for a personal website. I designed a bold and iconic red heart-shaped text logo featuring my initials, crafted in Adobe Illustrator as a vector graphic. This logo represents my identity and marks the first step in building my personal site for the course.
