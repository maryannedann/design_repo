This project was created for my Graphic Design for the Web course, where I was tasked with designing a personal logo and style tile to serve as the foundation for my personal website. I developed a bold, heart-shaped logo featuring my initials, crafted as a clean vector graphic in Adobe Illustrator. The logo’s strong red tone reflects my identity and creative energy. Complementing this, my style tile showcases a cohesive visual system with vibrant red and yellow hues, bold typography, and expressive font families that capture the overall tone of my personal brand.
